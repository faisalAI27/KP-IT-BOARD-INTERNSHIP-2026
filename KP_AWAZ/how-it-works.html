Skip to contribution process
From one voice to a trusted archive

Seven careful steps. One transparent contribution journey.

Your score is earned by administrator-approved recordings, never by submitting alone.

  1. 01

    Create an account

    Use a display name, email and password, or continue separately with Google.

  2. 02

    Verify a new email account

    Enter the six-digit code sent during signup. Returning password users do not repeat this step for normal sign-in.

  3. 03

    Choose a real recording task

    Read a guided Pashto sentence or provide the transcript for an open voice recording.

  4. 04

    Record, listen and submit

    Grant microphone permission, check playback, re-record if needed, then submit through the protected API.

  5. 05

    Wait for review

    Every new recording begins as Pending review. Pending recordings do not increase score.

  6. 06

    Receive a decision

    Approved recordings add one to your contribution score. Rejected recordings add zero and can include private feedback.

  7. 07

    Follow your impact

    View your private history, approved score, and public rank when leaderboard visibility is enabled.

The scoring rule

Contribution score = approved owned contributions.

Pending
0 score effect
Approved
+1 score
Rejected
0 score effect

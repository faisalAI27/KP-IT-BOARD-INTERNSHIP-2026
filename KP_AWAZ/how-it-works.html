Skip to contribution process
From one voice to a trusted collection

One clear contribution journey, from phrase to review.

See what the platform collects, why it matters, and what happens after you submit.

  1. 01

    Sign in securely

    Create an email-and-password account and verify it with the six-digit signup code, return with your password, or continue separately with Google.

  2. 02

    Receive a Pashto phrase

    The guided recording flow presents an active phrase selected from the project’s reviewed phrase collection.

  3. 03

    Record in your browser

    Allow microphone access and read the phrase naturally. The browser chooses a supported audio format for your device.

  4. 04

    Listen and re-record

    Play your recording back. If you want another take, re-record before you continue.

  5. 05

    Accept the current data-use policy

    The planned account-level step will ask you to review and explicitly accept the current policy once, then store that version securely with your account. This verified acceptance is not connected yet.

  6. 06

    Submit after acceptance is verified

    The studio currently supports recording, listening and re-recording, but upload is temporarily unavailable until account-level acceptance is connected. Once connected, original recorded bytes, the phrase reference and language will use the protected contribution route.

  7. 07

    See Pending review

    Every new contribution begins as Pending. Submission alone does not increase your score.

  8. 08

    Receive approval or rejection

    An administrator checks the contribution. Approved and Rejected results appear privately in My Contributions, with safe feedback when provided.

  9. 09

    Update score after approval

    An approved owned recording adds one to your contribution score. Pending and Rejected recordings add zero.

  10. 10

    Organize data for future research

    Eligible approved recordings remain connected to useful reference information and may later be prepared for research under the project’s consent and withdrawal policies.

Why context travels with the audio

A recording is more useful when its meaning and origin stay clear.

Phrase reference
Shows what the contributor was asked to read; it is not presented as a separately verified transcript.
Language
Keeps recordings organized with the language selected for that contribution.
Original audio format
Preserves exactly what the supported browser recorded. Stage A does not convert it for model training.
The scoring rule

Contribution score = approved owned contributions.

Pending
0 score effect
Approved
+1 score
Rejected
0 score effect

01

Language belongs to the people who live it.

Pashto and the regional languages of KP carry place names, family histories, humor, knowledge, and ways of speaking that generic datasets often miss.

Local recordings help language and speech technology understand real pronunciation and everyday expression instead of treating a community as an afterthought.

Review

Responsible by design

New contributions remain pending until an administrator reviews them. Only approved recordings affect public contribution scores.

Privacy

Identity stays private

Public rankings show an opted-in display name and approved score—never private email addresses, account identifiers, or storage paths.

Community

Built for shared benefit

Approved data may support language and speech technology. The aim is a durable, community-focused resource—not a collection of private account data.

Our mission

Spoken on every ridge and riverbank. Invisible to most AI.

From the valleys of Chitral to the plains of Peshawar, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa carries a dozen living tongues, most with almost no digital footprint. KP Awaz gathers the sentences, stories and speech samples needed to teach machines to listen in the languages people actually grew up speaking.

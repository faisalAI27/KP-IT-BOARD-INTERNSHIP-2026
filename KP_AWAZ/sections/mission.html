Our mission

A future where language is never a barrier.

Many people across Khyber Pakhtunkhwa are most comfortable communicating in Pashto and other local languages. However, most digital services and voice technologies are designed mainly around widely available English and Urdu data.

KP AWAZ exists to help close this gap. We are collecting real, diverse and voluntarily contributed voice recordings from the people of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa.

Our long-term goal is to support the development of technology that can understand local speech naturally—allowing people to communicate with digital systems without being limited by English, Urdu or difficult interfaces.

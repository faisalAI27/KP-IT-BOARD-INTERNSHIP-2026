Why it matters

When machines understand a language, more people can use them.

01

Millions of speakers, barely heard by AI

Speech and translation tools fail on frontier languages because representative datasets barely exist. Every recording narrows that gap.

02

Preservation through digitisation

Oral poetry, folk tales and dialect words can fade between generations. Recording them keeps that memory living and usable.

03

Technology built for this region

Better language AI means voice assistants, transcription and learning tools in the languages spoken at home.

Why it matters

Why does your voice matter?

Voice technology learns from examples of real human speech. When voices, accents and languages from a community are missing from the data, technology struggles to understand that community.

Every contribution helps represent the way people actually speak across Khyber Pakhtunkhwa. A larger and more diverse collection can help future systems understand regional accents, natural pronunciation and everyday speech more accurately.

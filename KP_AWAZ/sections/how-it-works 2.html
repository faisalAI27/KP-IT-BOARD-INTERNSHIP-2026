How it works

A few seconds of your voice can help shape the future.

1

Create your account

Sign in securely using email or Google.

2

Read a Pashto phrase

The platform provides a short phrase for you to read naturally.

3

Record your voice

Record, listen and re-record before submitting.

4

Administrator review

Your contribution stays Pending until an administrator checks it.

5

Support future technology

Approved recordings join the growing collection under the project’s consent and withdrawal policies.

What we are building

Building the foundation for local voice technology.

KP AWAZ is currently building the most important foundation: a trusted collection of real voice recordings connected to clearly defined Pashto phrases.

This platform securely collects, organizes and reviews voice contributions. Approved data may later support research and development of speech-recognition and voice-based systems.

  • 01Voice-based public services
  • 02Education and learning assistance
  • 03Healthcare guidance
  • 04Agriculture and farming support
  • 05Customer-service systems
  • 06Accessibility tools
  • 07Local-language digital assistants

These are long-term possibilities—not completed KP AWAZ products or guaranteed outcomes.

Trust and transparency

Your voice. Your choice.

Contributing to KP AWAZ is voluntary. Before submitting, contributors are shown how approved recordings may be used for language and speech-technology research and development.

Account information remains protected through the platform’s authentication and privacy controls. Recordings are reviewed, and contributors can use the supported withdrawal process when they no longer want a contribution included in future data use.

Account information is kept private and is not included in public phrase exports.
